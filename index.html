The Craft

From Grove to Glass

Selection
Handpicked from family groves outside Punta Cana

Only the finest sun-ripened Keitt and Kent mangoes make the cut. Each fruit is inspected at peak ripeness—when sugars and acidity reach perfect balance—ensuring exceptional aromatics in every batch.

100% Natural · Zero Additives
Fermentation
Small-batch natural fermentation

Fresh mango pulp ferments slowly over 10-14 days, developing complex flavor compounds. Temperature-controlled rooms maintain ideal conditions, allowing wild Caribbean yeasts to work their magic naturally.

10-14 Days · Natural Fermentation
Distillation
Double distilled in traditional copper alembic stills.

Through precise separation of heads, hearts, and tails, our master distillers preserve only the purest heart of the spirit—achieving crystal clarity, depth, and refined character.

Heart Cut Only · Premium Quality
Proofing
Gently proofed for balance and smoothness.

The spirit is brought to 40% ABV using pure, limestone-filtered water, preserving vibrant fruit notes while delivering a smooth, elegant finish.

40% ABV · Crystal Clarity

The Journey

Caribbean to Europe

Following the historic trade winds, each shipment traces the legendary Dominican-to-Europe passage.

Heritage Route

Inspired by historic 18th-century merchant voyages, our spirit follows the original Atlantic route connecting the Caribbean and Europe. This carefully controlled journey ensures gradual maturation, stable climate conditions, and a refined, well-balanced character.
